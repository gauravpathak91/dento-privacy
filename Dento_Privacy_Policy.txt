Dento (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a digital platform designed to help dental
clinics manage patient aligner treatment plans and send automated
reminders through WhatsApp. We are committed to protecting the privacy
of both dentists and patients who use our platform.

1.  Information We Collect 1.1 Dentist/Clinic Information

-   Full Name
-   Clinic Name
-   Contact Number
-   Email Address
-   Clinic Address
-   Login Credentials
-   Subscription & Billing Information

1.2 Patient Information (Added by the Dentist) - Full Name - Phone
Number - Age - Treatment Details (Aligner schedule, checkup dates) -
Assigned Dentist - Communication Preferences

1.3 Usage Information - App usage analytics - Login activity - Device
information (model, OS version, browser information)

1.4 WhatsApp Messaging Data We store: - Reminder schedules - Message
delivery logs We do not store the content of WhatsApp conversations
outside the automated messages sent by our system.

2.  How We Use the Information We use collected data for:

-   Providing Dento services
-   Managing aligner treatment schedules
-   Sending automated WhatsApp reminders
-   Improving app workflow and performance
-   Legal and audit requirements

3.  How We Send WhatsApp Messages Dento uses WhatsApp Business API to
    send:

-   Aligner tray change reminders
-   Checkup reminders We do not read or store personal WhatsApp
    messages.

4.  How We Store and Protect Data Your data is secured using:

-   Encryption
-   HTTPS
-   Access control
-   Secure authentication
-   Regular audits

Only authorized clinic staff can access patient data.

5.  Sharing of Information We do not sell your data. Information is only
    shared with:

-   WhatsApp API provider
-   Payment gateway providers
-   Cloud hosting providers

6.  Your Rights You may request:

-   Access
-   Correction
-   Deletion
-   Export of data Contact: gauravpathak91@gmail.com

7.  Data Retention

-   Dentist data retained while account is active
-   Patient data retained until deleted by clinic
-   Logs kept for 90–180 days

8.  Children’s Privacy All patient data is entered by dental
    professionals responsible for consent.
